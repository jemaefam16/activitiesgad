This website is about GAD (Gender and Development) related activities where people can register and attend the activities. 

Municipal Social Welfare and Development (MSWD) is a department or office within a municipality's local government unit that is responsible for administering social welfare and development programs and services to enhance the well-being of its residents. Social protection programs, child welfare, and protection services, gender and development programs, community development programs, and disaster risk reduction and management are all provided by the MSWD department. Its primary purpose is to promote community well-being and guarantee that vulnerable persons and families have access to the resources and support they require to improve their quality of life.

In the province of Romblon in the MIMAROPA region, Corcuera is a 5th-class municipality that is politically divided into 15 barangays, including Alegria, Ambulong, Colongcolong, Gobon, etc. 10,112 people were counted in Corcuera during the 2020 census, which was carried out by the Philippine Statistics Authority, and they are taken care of by the Municipal Social Welfare to uphold equality and awareness among all residents. 

MANDATE

  • As a basic provider of social programs and services, the MSWD office is commissioned to formulate and implement mandatory programs, projects and activities that promotes full protection and safety to the community specifically for the vulnerable and disadvantaged sectors.

VISION

  • A Municipality where the advantaged and vulnerable individuals, families and communities are empowered for an improved quality life.
  • Empowered individuals, families and communities in the Municipality of Corcuera, able to serve, protect and provide the needs of their families and communities towards an improved quality of life.

MISSION

  • To provide social protection and promote the rights and welfare of the poor, individuals, families and communities to contribute to poverty alleviation and empowerment through social welfare and development policies, developed appropriate programs, projects and services implementation in coordination with the GO’s, NGO’s and the private sector.
  • Intensify efforts to adapt effective measures for prompt delivery of basic services for all without bias and discrimination.

Organizational Outcome:
